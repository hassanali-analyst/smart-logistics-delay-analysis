## VERSION A — Insight-led (recommended)

🚚 What if I told you that in one logistics dataset, EVERY shipment caught in heavy traffic was delayed? 100%. No exceptions.

I just completed a data analysis project on a Smart Logistics dataset (1,000 shipments), and the result surprised me.

📊 What I found:
• 56.6% of all shipments were delayed
• Heavy traffic → 100% delay rate
• Clear / detour traffic → only ~35%
• Waiting time made almost no difference
• Truck choice was a minor factor

👉 The takeaway: traffic condition — not the truck, not waiting time — is the single biggest driver of late deliveries. The fix is operational: smarter scheduling and live traffic routing.

🛠️ Tools used: Excel (cleaning + PivotTables), SQL (analysis queries), Python (pandas + matplotlib), Power BI (dashboard).

💡 Biggest lesson: I almost trusted the "delay reason" column — until I tested it and found it was inconsistent. Always verify your data before you believe it.
